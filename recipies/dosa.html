Dosa

Description

Dosa is a popular South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. It is crispy and often served with a variety of chutneys and sambar. Dosas can be plain or stuffed with fillings like potato masala.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad Dal
  • Fenugreek Seeds
  • Salt
  • Water

Procedure

  1. Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately.
  2. Soak them in water for at least 6 hours.
  3. Grind the urad dal and fenugreek seeds to a smooth paste and the rice to a coarse paste.
  4. Mix both pastes and add salt. Let the batter ferment overnight.
  5. Heat a non-stick tawa and pour a ladleful of batter. Spread it thinly.
  6. Drizzle some oil and cook until crispy. Fold
