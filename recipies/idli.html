Idli

Description

Idli is a traditional breakfast in South Indian households. These are soft, fluffy rice cakes made by steaming a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. Idlis are usually served with chutney and sambar. This dish is known for its simplicity and nutritional value.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad Dal
  • Salt
  • Water

Procedure

  1. Wash the rice and urad dal separately.
  2. Soak them in water for at least 6 hours.
  3. Grind the urad dal to a smooth paste and the rice to a coarse paste.
  4. Mix both pastes and add salt. Let the batter ferment overnight.
  5. Pour the batter into idli molds and steam for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Remove the idlis from the molds and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
