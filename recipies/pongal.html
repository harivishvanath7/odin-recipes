Pongal

Description

Pongal is a popular South Indian rice dish, often served for breakfast or during festivals. It is made with rice and yellow moong dal, flavored with ghee, black pepper, and cumin. Pongal is traditionally served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Yellow Moong Dal
  • Ghee
  • Black Pepper
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Curry Leaves
  • Ginger
  • Salt

Procedure

  1. Wash the rice and moong dal together.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add the rice and moong dal with enough water.
  3. Add salt and cook for 3-4 whistles.
  4. In a pan, heat ghee and add black pepper, cumin seeds, curry leaves, and ginger.
  5. Pour this seasoning over the cooked rice and dal mixture. Mix well.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
